Stable Diffusion: black image output

Use this checklist in order. Stop when the error is resolved, and do not apply unrelated system changes from later steps.

Medium urgencyImage generation fix

Fast answer

1

Try a known compatible VAE.

2

Disable half precision for troubleshooting.

3

Test the same prompt with a different checkpoint.

Confirm the match

Symptoms

  • The generation completes but the image is black or nearly black.
  • The console may mention NaN or half precision.
  • Changing the prompt does not solve the issue.

Why it happens

Likely causes

  • The VAE is incompatible or missing.
  • The GPU and driver combination fails with half precision.
  • The model checkpoint is corrupted or not matched to the pipeline.

Diagnostics

Commands to check

python launch.py --no-halfpython launch.py --precision full --no-half

Safety note

Keep one baseline model and VAE for comparison.
