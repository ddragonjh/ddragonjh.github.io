Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Information we collect

Fix Atlas does not require an account. Server logs may include technical information such as requested URLs, browser type, IP address, and timestamps for security and reliability.

Analytics and advertising

Fix Atlas may use analytics and advertising services such as Google Analytics or Google AdSense after configuration. These services may use cookies or similar technologies to measure traffic, prevent abuse, personalize ads where allowed, and report ad performance.

Third-party vendors

Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this or other websites. Users can manage ad personalization in their Google ad settings. Google's advertising products may receive device, browser, approximate location, page, and ad-interaction information as described in Google's advertising policies.

Consent and privacy choices

Where required, a Google-certified consent message is used to obtain or record advertising choices for visitors in the EEA, United Kingdom, and Switzerland. Region-specific controls may also allow eligible United States visitors to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. Browser settings and advertising controls can be used to limit cookies or personalized ads.

Diagnostic tool

The error-message matcher runs in the visitor's browser. Text pasted into that tool is not intentionally transmitted to or stored by Fix Atlas. Visitors should still remove credentials, personal data, and private paths before pasting any log content.

Retention and children

Operational logs are retained only as reasonably needed for security, reliability, and aggregate measurement. Fix Atlas is a general developer reference and is not directed to children under 13.
