Why it is different

A verification workflow, not another answer list

Error search pages usually stop after suggesting a command. Debug Lab records the failure signature before the change and identifies what disappeared, survived, or appeared afterward. That makes a repair testable instead of merely plausible.

The handoff generator preserves the useful evidence while removing common credentials, tokens, email addresses, and local usernames. Always review the result before publishing because no automatic sanitizer can recognize every private value.

Security basis

Review logs before sharing them

The redaction categories follow OWASP guidance on excluding access tokens, passwords, connection strings, encryption keys, session identifiers, and sensitive personal data from logs.
