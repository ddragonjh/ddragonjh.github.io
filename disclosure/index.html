Transparency / last updated July 20, 2026

Advertising and sponsorship disclosure

Fix Atlas is built as a free technical reference. Advertising may support its hosting, research, maintenance, and continued expansion.

Display advertising

The site uses or may use Google AdSense to display advertising. Advertisements are selected and delivered by Google and its partners, not written as Fix Atlas recommendations. Advertising does not change the troubleshooting order, safety notes, or choice of official source.

Affiliate links

Fix Atlas does not currently publish paid affiliate links. If an affiliate relationship is introduced, qualifying links and affected pages will be clearly labeled before the link. A commission will not increase the reader's price.

Sponsorships

Sponsored material, if accepted, will be labeled as sponsored and separated from independent repair instructions. Payment does not buy a favorable technical conclusion, removal of accurate criticism, or an undocumented product claim.

Questions

Questions about a commercial relationship or a page's independence can be sent to the address on the contact page. Corrections are handled under the editorial policy regardless of advertising status.
